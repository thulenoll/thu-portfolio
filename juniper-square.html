About Juniper Square

Juniper Square is the operating system for private markets — connected software and services that run the full lifecycle of a private fund, from fundraising to final distribution. Its customers are general partners across real estate, private equity, venture, and private credit, who use it to unify fundraising, fund accounting, compliance, and investor reporting in one place instead of a dozen disconnected tools. Today it supports more than 2,300 GPs managing roughly $1 trillion in investor equity, with AI increasingly built into fund operations.

02 — In motion

Cut down, again and again, until the interaction is clear.

Much of the year went into cutting busy screens down to the one thing each piece needs to show. Six examples below — dashboards, AI drafting, investor comments, the investor portal, staff review flow, and the human checks built into JunieAI. Each is a real moment from the product, trimmed to what a viewer can take in at a glance. I added animation touches to the product graphics so the story reads clearer.

04 — Working with AI

A few months of making everything with AI.

Lately the whole process runs with AI in the loop — storyboards, copy, layout, production. The clearest piece of it is the brand-system foundation I built for Juniper Square inside Claude: the same visual rules, written so Claude can read them — so the team can make on-brand marketing in Claude Design and the Claude plugin, all from one place.
